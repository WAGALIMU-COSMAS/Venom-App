School Mission

To teach and educate needy children in the fishing communities and to provide quality education to the left out communities in the Lake Victoria Uganda.

School Vission

See neglected and vulnerable children leading this country throuogh quality education and to be a leading first world school in the fishing community.

School Core Values

Fearing God, Respect and Discipline, Compassion, Integrity, Environmental Protection, Sanitation and Hygiene, Expression in English, Self Sustenance through Agriculture.


School Background
Island Mission Uganda project with a mission to improve the life of the fisher forks through quality services and after realising all the gaps the children go through in the fishing communities.
In 2013, we started the mission of giving quality education to the needy to the children in a fishing communities in Uganda.
We started by making children outreach to 16 Islands creating children days(Queen's and King's days) to make the neglected and vulnerable children feel loved and cared for.
In 2016, Island Mision Uganda started MOUNT EAGLE JUNIOR SCHOOL ZZINGAto accomplish the objectives of community improvement through providing quality education in this fishing community.
In a bid to establish continuity of the quality education from Mount Eagle Junior, in 2020, MOUNT EAGLE HIGH SCHOOL.
The school is located on ZZinga Island, Wakiso District.
Mount Eagle High School is popular for it's quality, sustainable and talents promoting education.
